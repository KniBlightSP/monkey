Monkeys

Monkeys are primates that belong to the suborder Haplorhini. They are known for their intelligence, agility, and their ability to climb trees. Monkeys can be found in various parts of the world, including Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

There are different species of monkeys, such as the chimpanzee, orangutan, gorilla, and baboon. Monkeys are social animals and often live in groups called troops. They have a diverse diet that includes fruits, leaves, insects, and sometimes small animals.

Monkeys are often depicted in popular culture, appearing in books, movies, and art. They have been a subject of fascination for humans for centuries, and their behavior and characteristics continue to be studied by scientists.
